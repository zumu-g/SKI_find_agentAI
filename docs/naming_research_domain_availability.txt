KEY COMPETITORS ALREADY OPERATING IN THIS SPACE:
- GetAgent.co.uk - major player since 2015, 1.2M+ homeowners served, 8,000+ agents
- BestAgent.co.uk - active, video introductions from local agents
- CompareAgents.uk - active comparison tool
- Compare-Agents.com - active, real-time sales data

- SellMyHome.co.uk - online estate agent since 2013, RICS regulated
- OnlineAgentPicker.com - comparison site
- CompareEstateAgents.which.co.uk - Which? branded
- CompareOnlineAgents.co.uk - active
- CompareEstatesAgents.co.uk - active
- CompareStateAgents.co.uk - active
- Swoople.com - agent comparison
- OneDome.com - find & compare agents
- AllAgents.co.uk - reviews platform

SUCCESSFUL UK COMPARISON/MARKETPLACE BRAND NAME PATTERNS:
- Compound descriptive: MoneySupermarket, CompareTheMarket, GoCompare
- Action verb: Checkatrade, Confused.com, uSwitch
- Abstract/invented: Zoopla (property), Yopa (Your Online Property Agent), Emoov
- These brands dominate through familiarity - 62.2% of users return because they used them before

WHAT MAKES A GOOD NAME (from research):
- Short, memorable, easy to spell and pronounce
- Evocative of the brand purpose
- Scalable (won't outgrow the name)
- Ideally includes or implies a high-traffic keyword
- For UK: .co.uk builds trust, .com builds global credibility

================================================================================
NOTES FOR NAMING COMMITTEE
================================================================================

1. The "agent comparison" space in the UK is EXTREMELY crowded. There are 10+ active
   comparison sites already using variations of "compare" + "agent". Avoid this pattern.

2. GetAgent.co.uk is the dominant brand in the exact space Kova targets. Any name
   containing "agent" + action verb risks confusion.

3. The most defensible names are likely ABSTRACT/INVENTED (like Zoopla, Yopa) or
   use UNEXPECTED COMBINATIONS (like Checkatrade, MoneySupermarket).

4. AI-focused names are trending in 2026 proptech ("Homes AI", "Agentic PropTech")
   but risk dating quickly if AI hype fades.

5. UK consumers strongly trust .co.uk domains. Securing both .com and .co.uk is ideal.

6. The successful UK comparison brands (GoCompare, CompareTheMarket, Confused.com,
   MoneySupermarket) all share these traits:
   - Immediately understandable purpose
   - Memorable and slightly playful
   - Work well in advertising/word-of-mouth
   - Most are compound words that didn't exist before the brand

7. ALL candidates above require formal domain availability checks via a registrar
   and UK trademark searches via the IPO before shortlisting. Web search alone
   cannot confirm true availability.
