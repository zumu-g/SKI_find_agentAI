## 1. Marketing Strategy Overview

### Mission Statement

Kova exists to remove the guesswork from choosing an estate agent. We empower UK homeowners with transparent, data-driven insights so they can sell their home with confidence -- choosing an agent based on verified performance, not flashy marketing.

### Strategic Objectives (Year 1)

| Objective | Target | Timeline |
|-----------|--------|----------|
| Monthly unique website visitors | 50,000 | Month 12 |
| Monthly qualified leads (homeowners entering postcode) | 3,000 | Month 12 |
| Conversion to agent instruction | 15% of leads | Ongoing |
| Agent partners onboarded | 500+ agencies | Month 12 |
| Cost per acquisition (homeowner lead) | Under GBP 12 | Month 6 onwards |
| Brand awareness (prompted, target postcode areas) | 15% | Month 12 |

### Go-To-Market Approach

Kova operates a two-sided marketplace. The marketing strategy prioritises demand-side (homeowner) acquisition, since agent supply follows demand. The core flywheel:

1. **Attract homeowners** through SEO, PPC, content, and referrals
2. **Deliver value** via free, transparent agent comparison powered by Land Registry data
3. **Convert** homeowners into agent instructions (generating referral revenue)
4. **Build advocacy** through exceptional outcomes, prompting word-of-mouth and reviews

The revenue model (0.25% referral fee capped at GBP 2,500+VAT on successful sale) means Kova only earns when a homeowner successfully sells. This alignment of incentives is a powerful trust signal and should be central to all marketing.

## 3. Brand Positioning & Messaging Framework

### Brand Positioning Statement

For UK homeowners who want to sell their property with confidence, Kova is the AI-powered estate agent comparison platform that uses verified Land Registry data to match you with the best-performing local agent. Unlike relying on a friend's recommendation or an agent's self-reported claims, Kova provides transparent, data-backed evidence of real agent performance.

### Brand Personality

| Trait | What it means in practice |
|-------|---------------------------|
| **Warm** | We speak like a knowledgeable friend, not a corporation. First person plural. Contractions are fine. |
| **Trustworthy** | We lead with data and transparency. We never oversell or make claims we can't back up. |
| **Empowering** | We give homeowners the information to make their own decision. We guide, not push. |
| **Grounded** | We're practical and down-to-earth. No jargon, no hype. |
| **Modern** | We embrace technology but always in service of simplicity, never complexity for its own sake. |

### Brand Voice Guidelines

**Tone:** Conversational, confident, supportive. Think "trusted neighbour who happens to be a property expert."

**Do:**
- Use plain English ("We check how agents actually perform" not "We leverage proprietary algorithmic analysis")
- Address the reader directly ("you", "your home")
- Acknowledge that selling is emotional as well as financial
- Use data to reassure, not to overwhelm
- Be specific ("Agents in SW11 sell in an average of 34 days" not "We have lots of data")

**Don't:**
- Use aggressive sales language ("Act now!", "Don't miss out!")
- Talk down to the reader or assume ignorance
- Use estate agency jargon without explanation
- Make absolute guarantees ("We guarantee you'll get a higher price")
- Sound corporate or robotic

**Writing samples:**

> "Choosing the right estate agent can mean thousands of pounds difference on your sale price. We make that choice easier -- with real data from real sales, not marketing promises."

> "We're free for homeowners. Always. Agents pay us a small referral fee only when your sale completes. That means we're on your side from start to finish."

### Key Messages by Persona

| Persona | Primary Message | Supporting Proof Point |
|---------|----------------|----------------------|
| First-Time Seller Fiona | "Take the stress out of selling your first home" | Step-by-step guidance + verified agent data = confidence |
| Downsizer Derek | "Find an agent worthy of your home" | Local expertise rankings backed by Land Registry records |
| Relocator Raj | "The fastest way to find the right agent" | 60-second matching + days-on-market data + remote-friendly |
| Investment Seller Imani | "Data-driven decisions for data-driven sellers" | Fee comparisons, sale price achieved vs asking, portfolio tools |

### Taglines and Value Propositions

**Primary Tagline:**
> "Find your best agent. Backed by real data."

**Alternative Taglines (for testing):**
> "The smarter way to choose your estate agent."
> "Every home deserves the right agent."
> "Sell smarter. Sell with confidence."

**Core Value Propositions:**

1. **Data you can trust.** Kova's recommendations are powered by verified Land Registry sales data -- not agent marketing or paid placements.
2. **Completely free for homeowners.** We only earn when your sale completes. Our interests are aligned with yours.
3. **Matched in seconds.** Enter your postcode and get a shortlist of top-performing agents in under 60 seconds.
4. **AI-powered, human-centred.** Our technology analyses millions of data points so you don't have to. The result? A clear, simple comparison.
5. **No pressure, no obligation.** Compare agents, read the data, and decide in your own time.

### 5.3 Referral Programme Design

**"Recommend Kova" Programme:**

- **Mechanic:** Existing users who have completed a sale through Kova can refer friends/family
- **Incentive:** GBP 50 Amazon gift card for the referrer when the referred person instructs an agent through Kova. Referred person receives a free "Pre-Sale Home Audit Checklist" pack.
- **Tracking:** Unique referral links generated in user dashboard
- **Promotion:** Post-completion email, thank-you page, social sharing buttons
- **Target:** 10% of completed users refer at least one person
- **Expected CAC via referral:** GBP 50 (gift card) -- well below paid channel CAC

**"Agent Referral" Programme:**

- Agents who are Kova partners can refer homeowners (who are selling a second property or know someone selling) back into the platform
- Agent receives priority placement weighting (not fee reduction) for active referrals
- Encourages agents to view Kova as a partner, not a threat

## 12. Growth Hacking Tactics

### Tactic 1: The "Street-Level Challenge"

**Concept:** Create hyper-local social content: "We analysed every sale on [specific street] in the last 5 years. Here's what we found."

**Execution:**
- Use Land Registry data to create micro-analyses of popular/recognisable streets
- Share as Instagram carousels, TikTok videos, and X posts
- Tag local community groups and pages
- Encourage homeowners to request their own street analysis (email gate)

**Expected outcome:** High shareability, local virality, 500+ email captures per month from Month 3

**Cost:** Staff time only (data is already available)

### Tactic 2: "The Agent Transparency Report"

**Concept:** Publish a quarterly, free-to-access report ranking estate agent chains and independents by verified performance metrics (days on market, sale-to-asking ratio, market share).

**Execution:**
- Create a shareable, data-rich report with clear rankings
- Pitch to property press as an exclusive pre-release
- Agents who perform well will share it (free PR for them), creating organic distribution
- Poor performers create controversy (drives engagement and press coverage)

**Expected outcome:** 10+ media mentions per report, 2,000+ downloads, establishes Kova as the definitive source for agent performance data

**Cost:** GBP 500/quarter (design + distribution)

### Tactic 3: Product-Led SEO (Programmatic Pages)

**Concept:** Create thousands of auto-generated, data-rich pages targeting long-tail searches.

**Pages to create:**
- kova.ai/best-estate-agents/[town] (200+ towns)
- kova.ai/house-prices/[postcode] (2,000+ postcodes)
- kova.ai/estate-agent/[agent-name]-review (1,000+ agents)

**Execution:**
- Template-based pages populated from Land Registry data
- Each page has unique data, charts, and insights
- Internal linking between related pages (town -> agents in town -> postcode data)
- Updated monthly with fresh data (Google rewards freshness)

**Expected outcome:** 10,000+ organic visits/month by Month 12 from long-tail keywords. This becomes the dominant acquisition channel in Year 2.

**Cost:** GBP 2,000 development investment + ongoing data processing

### Tactic 4: The "What's Your Agent Really Like?" Widget

**Concept:** Create an embeddable widget that property bloggers and journalists can place on their sites. The widget lets visitors enter a postcode and instantly see their top 3 agents.

**Execution:**
- Build a lightweight, brandable widget (< 50KB)
- Offer to property bloggers, comparison sites, and local news sites
- Each widget impression and click-through builds Kova's brand and captures leads
- Provide affiliate revenue share for sites that drive completed instructions

**Expected outcome:** 20+ sites embedding the widget by Month 9, driving 1,000+ leads/month

**Cost:** GBP 1,500 development investment

### Tactic 5: "Sold Price Alert" Notification Service

**Concept:** Free email/SMS alert when a property sells on your street or in your postcode. Similar to a price-drop alert for products, but for property sales.

**Execution:**
- User enters postcode and email
- Automated alerts sent when new Land Registry completions are recorded nearby
- Each alert includes: "Wondering what your home is worth? Compare agents in your area"
- Builds a large engaged email list of property-interested homeowners (future sellers)

**Expected outcome:** 5,000+ subscribers by Month 6 (low barrier, high value to user). 10% convert to active Kova users within 12 months.

**Cost:** GBP 1,000 development + email sending costs

### Tactic 6: Strategic Reddit & Forum Presence

**Concept:** Build genuine credibility on property forums where homeowners ask for advice.

**Platforms:** Reddit (r/HousingUK, r/UKPersonalFinance), Mumsnet Property, MSE Forums

**Execution:**
- Create accounts that genuinely contribute helpful advice (not spam)
- When relevant, mention Kova as one resource among several
- Create "AMA" style posts: "I built a tool that analyses every estate agent's real performance. AMA."
- Share interesting data findings from Kova's analysis

**Expected outcome:** Steady trickle of high-intent, low-cost traffic. 200-500 visits/month by Month 6. Extremely high conversion rate (5-10%) due to warm context.

**Cost:** Staff time only (1-2 hours/week)

### Tactic 7: The "We'll Pay Your Agent's Fee" Competition

**Concept:** Monthly prize draw where one Kova user wins their estate agent fee paid (up to GBP 3,000).

**Execution:**
- Entry: Use Kova to compare agents and instruct one through the platform
- Monthly winner announced on social media (with permission)
- Creates urgency, social proof, and PR opportunities
- Total cost is predictable and modest

**Expected outcome:** 15% increase in instruction conversion rate, regular social media content, local press coverage

**Cost:** GBP 3,000/month -- but only runs from Month 6 when revenue starts. Self-funding from referral fees.

### Tactic 8: "Ask an Expert" Live Sessions

**Concept:** Weekly live Q&A on Instagram/Facebook where Kova's team answers selling questions.

**Execution:**
- 30-minute live session every Thursday at 7pm
- Topics rotate: choosing agents, pricing your home, preparing to sell, understanding the market
- Viewers submit questions in advance (email capture) and live
- Sessions are recorded and repurposed as YouTube videos, blog posts, and social clips

**Expected outcome:** 100+ live viewers by Month 4, 500+ views on recorded content, positions Kova as a trusted authority

**Cost:** Staff time only
